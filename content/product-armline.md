# ArmLine Product Family

The ArmLine 100 is Acme Robotics' entry-level collaborative robotic arm. It has a 5 kg payload capacity, a reach of 850 mm, and repeatability of ±0.03 mm. It is designed for tasks like small parts pick-and-place, light assembly, and quality inspection. The ArmLine 100 ships with a standard two-finger gripper, though customers can swap in vacuum or three-finger grippers from Acme's accessory catalog.

The ArmLine 500 is the heavy-duty sibling in the family. It supports a 25 kg payload, a reach of 1300 mm, and is rated for continuous 24/7 operation on factory floors. The ArmLine 500 is commonly used for palletizing, machine tending, and welding support. Unlike the ArmLine 100, the 500 requires a dedicated 220V power supply and floor-mounted installation.

Both arms run on AcmeOS, which provides a drag-and-drop programming interface as well as a Python SDK for advanced users. Firmware updates are pushed automatically when the arm is connected to the local network, unless auto-update is disabled in settings.

Standard warranty on the ArmLine 100 is 2 years; the ArmLine 500 ships with a 3-year warranty due to its industrial-duty rating. Extended warranty plans of up to 5 years are available for both models.
